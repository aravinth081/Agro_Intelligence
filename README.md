**Agro Intelligence** is a comprehensive React-based Single Page Application (SPA) designed to empower smallholder farmers. By bridging the gap between raw data and actionable intelligence, this platform helps farmers proactively mitigate climatic, biological, and financial risks while optimizing profitability. 

---

## 🌍 Social & Economic Impact
* **Zero Middlemen Marketplace:** A direct-to-consumer B2B model that eliminates the 30%-40% broker commissions, maximizing farmer profits.
* **Financial Protection:** Proactive disease detection and predictive risk simulation save farmers from devastating yield losses.
* **Financial Inclusion:** Automated loan processing breaks down bureaucratic barriers, making government subsidies accessible to all.
* **Digital Literacy:** A seamless bilingual interface (Tamil/English) ensures advanced AI is accessible regardless of technical education.

---

## ✨ 8 Core Modules

1. 🧑‍🌾 **Farmer Digital Twin:** A hyper-personalized profile (land size, soil type, crop history, risk tolerance) that calibrates all AI recommendations to the specific farmer.
2. 🎛️ **Command Center Dashboard:** A live telemetry hub displaying real-time weather (via OpenWeatherMap API) and dynamic AI Action Plans.
3. 🔬 **AI Crop Doctor:** A virtual botanical pathologist. Farmers upload leaf images for instant biometric scanning, root cause analysis, and dual treatment plans (chemical & organic).
4. ⚙️ **What-If Risk Simulator:** An interactive predictive analytics tool. Simulates stressors (rainfall drop, temperature spikes) to calculate Integrated Risk Scores and preemptive mitigation strategies.
5. 🛒 **Agri Marketplace (P2P):** A dual-sided e-commerce platform to buy agricultural inputs and sell harvests directly. Features a functional cart, UPI QR checkout, and automated PDF invoices.
6. 🌐 **Smart Market Intelligence:** A geo-spatial analytics engine that compares transport overheads and local demand between two districts to find the most lucrative market.
7. 🏦 **Loan Access Portal:** A streamlined gateway for micro-financing featuring simulated automated underwriting and instant downloadable Provisional Sanction Letters.
8. 💬 **AgroBot & AI Text Enhancer:** A conversational UI for instant farming queries and an NLP-driven text enhancer to refine user feedback submissions.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Styling & UI:** Tailwind CSS, Lucide-React Icons
* **Data Visualization:** Recharts
* **Document Generation:** jsPDF & html2pdf.js
* **Live Telemetry API:** OpenWeatherMap API
* **Vision AI API:** Google Gemini Vision API / Plant.id (For real-world leaf diagnostics)
